This week, we made lots of progress on our updated mechanical design. Anders designed roamr V2, an upgrade from roamr V1 originally designed by Nathan.

Here, we decided to maintain the burger design of V1, but added more space between the two panels to slot the battery inside as well as the electronics. Furthermore, we added a optical breadboard-like mounting hole pattern on the two panels, making it easy to shift the mounting of different components around and add new parts.
